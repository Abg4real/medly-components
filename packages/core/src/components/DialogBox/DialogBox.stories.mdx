# DialogBox

Basic DialogBox component. You can play with the component in the canvas tab.

DialogBox consists of three sections Header, Content and Actions. All of these you can directly use from DialogBox component only like bellow . . .

```typescript
<DialogBox open={openState} onCloseDialogBox={changeDialogBoxState(false)}>
    <DialogBox.Header>Add User</DialogBox.Header>
    <DialogBox.Content>
        <Input id="name-input" type="text" fullWidth label="Name" placeholder="Enter your Name" />
    </DialogBox.Content>
    <DialogBox.Actions>
        <Button variant="outlined">Cancel</Button>
    </DialogBox.Actions>
</DialogBox>
```

Shadow will appear on the Header & Actions section if you scroll the content.
To close the dialog-box you can perform below actions:

1. Click on the cross icon
2. Press `Esc` key
3. If you have provided `shouldCloseOnOutsideClick` this prop then dialog-box will close on clicking outside of dialog-box
